About Us
The EMGChair is built to cheaply aid people in emergency situations, especially in times of natural disaster. In Canada, flooding is a common issue and the victims often end up in emergency shelters. Unfortunately, there they are often limited to the floor, which is uncomfortable and can add to the stress of the situation.

The EMGChair is built to be comfortable and easy to set up. Due to the situation, the chair also needs to be easy to ship and stay inexpensive as people may suffer financial damages. As many of these chairs will be produced, they must also be recyclable to minimize environmental footprint.

It is created by a group of 8 dedicated students from SHAD Valley, motivated to minimize the negative impacts of natural disasters.
