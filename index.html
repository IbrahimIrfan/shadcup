About Us

DriveBud was created to help change the amount of fuel an individual unconsciously wastes while driving. Individuals need to know that changing their habits a little bit will save a lot over time, and our product pushes you to apply this knowledge.

Our product works with an app to easily notifies you of how you can change your driving habits to be more eco-friendly. Not only does our app show you how you have had an impact on the environment from your individual driving, but we will also plant a tree for every 100 litres of gas you save.

DriveBud was created by a group of 8 bright students who met at SHAD Valley and had the opportunity to work together to create this environmentally friendly business. They created this business with a vision of reducing the individual energy footprint of all Canadians through efficient and informed driving.
